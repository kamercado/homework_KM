Crime in LA (Project 1)

This project was to explore the characteristics of crime in LA.

Questions Project Addressed

  • What types of arrests in LA are most common?
  • Has crime in LA increased or decreased over time?
  • What time of day are arrests in LA more common?
  • What category of arrests/crimes are common in particular areas of LA? (by Area Name)
  • Areas with most/least crimes?
  • What types of crimes are committed most by certain age groups?
  • What types of crimes are committed most between males and females?

About the Data Used

The data used in the project was pulled from Kaggle.com and is hosted by the City of Los Angeles.

A link to the dataset can be found here.

The dataset contains information on arrests made between 2010-2019. Information the dataset presents includes:

  • Report ID
  • Arrest date
  • Time of arrest
  • Location of arrest (Area ID/Name, District, Address, Zip Code)
  • Age, sex, descent (of person arrested)
  • Charge Description (Reason for arrest)

Scope

This website will highlight the demographics of those who have been arrested for crimes in LA.
